Fellow

./source/index.js

A fellow with similarties to other people.

Also contains the properties:

  • {String} homepage
  • {String} githubUrl
  • {String} githubUsername
  • {String} twitterUrl
  • {String} twitterUsername
  • {String} facebookUrl
  • {String} facebookUsername
